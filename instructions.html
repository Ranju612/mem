Instructions

Learn how to play Memory Garden with simple, step-by-step guidance. Understand the rules, controls, and scoring system to maximize your fun.

  • 🎮 Use arrow keys or on-screen buttons to move your character.
  • 💡 Reach the glowing exit to finish each level.
  • ⚡ Each level lasts 2 minutes, so move quickly!
  • 🏆 Your score increases as you finish levels faster.
